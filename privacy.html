Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Job Filter

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Simple Summary: This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. Everything happens locally in your browser.

Data Collection

LinkedIn Job Filter does not collect any user data. The extension:

  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not access your LinkedIn profile information
  • Does not collect or store any personal information
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools

Local Storage

The extension stores only your filter preferences (which types of jobs to hide: applied, promoted, or reposted) using Chrome's local storage API. This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted over the network
  • Is only used to remember your filter settings
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Used to save your filter preferences locally
  • activeTab: Used to detect when you're on a LinkedIn jobs page
  • linkedin.com access: Required to read job card attributes and apply filters

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics tools.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension listing and GitHub repository.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
